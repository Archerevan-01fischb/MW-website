FOR ADVANCED TESTERS: F9 TUNING PANEL

Advanced feature for experienced testers. Press F9 to open the real-time parameter tuning window. Press P to pause while you tweak.

  • Enemy Vehicles - Movement & Detection
  • Weapons - Torpedoes, Missiles, Sniper Rifle
  • Rendering - Cull Distance, Fog settings
  • Radar - Range, Cycle times, Thresholds

Save to File, Reset to Defaults, or Reload - works in standalone builds!
